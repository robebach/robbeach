You need to go past a good idea to find novel solutions.
Mistakes are just branches that went the wrong way.
Jesus said, a seed scattered on concrete will not grow.
But when you don't know you don't know.
And the way the world works is to seek new routes out
Growing our understanding of what's possible as we prod the depths
Laid out before our species on this planet
Who have limited eyes to unravel its complexity
But those who work loosely like the filaments of a fine flower
Can open chasms through pinholes, worlds behind doors.
Alice was able to discover an imagination through a locked door
She simply had to figure out how to open it.
Sometimes we can find doors that seem to have something special behind them
Only to find more when you get through. 
Or the stranded who when cresting a hill only finds it was only the first forever
Hope springs eternal, only after we've realized the facts of life
That some things fail, and the more we understand how we fit into the world
The more we can thrive. 

We have to act on that understanding. Knowing without action


The infinite possibilities in a potent universal structure
Our own local network is dirty, as it should be. It can't be any other way.
If we stop looking for ways to tie the world together, reconfigure, reshape 
Without destroying undiscovered possibilities
We will no longer exist, with all meristems cut
Those special units at the vanguard of discovery
