I like to think of myself like a seed carried by the wind.
I have my DNA and little else, 
and I will land and create a plant.

Like a drifter, but not alone. 
Parasitic loosely
Less assertive than most, but open to the unknown.
Our best asset is the world, not our world. 
Our world is small.
