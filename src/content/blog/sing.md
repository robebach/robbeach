I get the sense sometimes, like an instinct
That there is some cohesive or networked narrative that describes me or my life.
Could be my mantra or my title, or a name with implications. 
The title can not be known until it is done, and with
no time like the present I make my way through switching ideas. No time
to think, just do. But so far I haven't done what I think is possible. 
How is it myself gets in the way of myself, preventing me 
from placing my narrative on teh page or letting other people in on the secret. 
I must learn to sing.
