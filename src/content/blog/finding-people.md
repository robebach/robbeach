finding people
someone is out of reach, but important
the concept is this: you have to meet them where they are
it's not only a physical place
empathy means going there, and it's a skill
going there is to try to enter their world
which is entirely separate from your own
not exactly like two minecraft worlds, but sort of
We can't know what's going on in their head
except we can do a pretty good job if we try
It's not a magical connection, it is a mental one like a mind meld, but more one directional
Perhaps its a gradient like everything else, but way more complex because
it encompases our minds which surely are the final frontier for any species.
