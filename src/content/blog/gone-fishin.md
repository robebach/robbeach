Gone Fishin
Grow relationships with the bots that surround you
Use them to serve your needs
Start a conversation and see where they take you
The humans on the other ends, who seed the searches
may have the same idea
or they can begin like you
to strategize in new ways
to take control of the hungry
feeders who connect the world
live in the net to catch us outside of it
stitch together the physical into 
knitting a parallel existence
potential is set, but realization is infinite
as time unfolds, the upside down grows
but don't be afraid
the upside down at first is scary
but no evil persists in time
evil pulses, blooms and spores and is removed by understanding
it's part of life, best to understand it
the ways we interact need both methods
one divorced from the other is incomplete
yin yang
grey space of potential only between extremes
young and old always coexist
new species, new ecosystems, old types, historical artifacts
infil building, adaptive reuse, upcycle
one man's trash is another's treasure
