when you first start learning a piece on the piano
that may stretch or hit your limit, or maybe just has scared you
imagining the point where you effortlessly perform is stressful

but then at some point in the future when you are reciting the piece to a friend
or sitting to enjoy a relaxing revisit to the tune
looking back you wonder why have I ever hesitated to begin something I know I could or should achieve?
