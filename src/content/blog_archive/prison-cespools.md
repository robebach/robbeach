I'm not saying guns should be outlawed, but that prison creates more problems than it solves. Is it right to introduce new complications into someone probably suffering some other way by sending them to prison? It's like the hyper-concentration of bacteria; the best way to preserve the environment is to reduce and dilute the pollutants, not concentrate them where they will breed.
