These things should go alongside windows not replace them, Anthony. While I know you think humans should live in robotic boxes controlled by science, humans love to be connected to experiences had outside which just so happen to be irreplaceable by technology. Havent you seen Interstellar, man?  There has always been a problem of human happiness in brutally windowless buildings like nuclear reactors since it was realized, to be happy, humans need the sun and all that comes with it. And I definitely want one of these in my kitchen. Seems like this new tech gives us a programmable window with light to match that of a real window from a place far away and sunnier, but why have a light when its nice outside? (or an HVAC system for that matter) I dont think its a solution to replace our connection to the outside with digital interfaces that mimic that experience, I think we need to be grounded in reality, man.
